About Us

The team behind the dream

The Turkey Team is a group of dedicated individuals who are passionate about providing Thanksgiving meals to those in need. Our mission is to ensure that everyone has the opportunity to enjoy a warm meal during the holiday season, regardless of their circumstances. Inspired by a similar project completed at his local church, The Turkey Team began when president and founder, Chase Black, saw the needs in his community go unmet. In 2019, with the help of his family and local church members, Chase was able to comeplete his Eagle Scout project by collecting donations, acquiring food, assembling baskets, and delivering them to 25 families in his community. In 2023, Chase gathered a team of volunteers (his college friends) to start The Turkey Team, an organization dedicated to giving back to those in need.


Chase, Spencer, Cole, and Grace with our favorite Walmart employee, Deana, who helped us check out all this food!

As a non-profit organization that works with local school districts and social workers, we have been able to reach more families that are in need of assistance. In 2023, we were able to provide 11 families with Thanksgiving meals. In 2024, we were able to provide 22 families with Thanksgiving meals, and sponsored a family for Christmas. This year, we are aiming to double our impact and provide meals to 44 families in need. We are committed to making a positive impact in our community and ensuring that no one goes hungry during a time where family and gratitude are so important.
