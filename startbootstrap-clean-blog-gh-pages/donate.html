How to help

The Turkey Team is a non-profit organization that relies on the generosity of individuals and businesses to help us provide Thanksgiving meals to families in need. Your donations make a significant difference in the lives of those we serve. Here are some ways you can help:

  1. Venmo
    • One of the easiest ways is to scan the code below and donate what you can! We take all the money raised, calculate how much of each item we can get, go to the local grocery store, and use ALL the funds to buy food.
  2. Donating Food
    • We welcome anyone that can donate food! What we can take: canned corn, canned green beans, butter, 2L Coke-A-Cola Soda Bottle, Idahoan Mashed Potatoes, Cool Whip, and 5lb bag of potatoes.
  3. Sharing With Friends and Family
    • Find us on Instagram @The_Turkey_Team
    • Share our posts and our message with family and friends! It's the best way to get as much help as possible!
  4. Volunteering
    • We are always looking for volunteers to help us collect donations, assemble baskets, and deliver meals. If you are interested in volunteering, please contact us!

Thank you for your support! Together, we can make a difference in the lives of families in need this Thanksgiving season.
